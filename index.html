Welcome to the Taco Hangout!

About Us

Welcome to the Taco Hangout amigos! We are a street taco restaurant chain based out of Moreno Valley, California. Established in 2013, we are known for our Frozen Habanero Margaritas, laid back vibes and of course the massive variety of tacos we sell! Please stop in and try our new Limited Time Poptart Lobster Taco! Don't forget to wash it down with one of our famous Baja Blast Horchatas!
